Moana

Who is Moana?

Moana is the daughter of the chief of the island of Motunui. Moana is vibrant, tenacious, and optimistic. She loves the sea, is strong-willed, and ultimately she is fearless. However, her love for the sea isn't shared by the people of her village. Being devided between her devotion as chief-in-training, and her dream to voyage the sea, Moana faces a lot of challenges within herself.
When a darkness threatens all life on the island. Moana is chosen by the sea to sail out and save her island, together with demi-god Maui. In this mission she can finally unify her love for her people with her dream to voyage like her ancestors once did before her
